# Prospector's Instinct Design Notes

## Core Philosophy

The mod should never feel like a GPS.

The player should feel like they are following an instinct.

Particles are guidance, not instructions.

When the player is directly above an ore deposit,
the guidance should become more subtle and immersive
instead of pointing underground.

Every visual effect should answer one question:

"What would make the player naturally understand where to dig?"

## Guidance System

The guidance should behave differently depending on the player's relationship to the nearest ore.

Searching
- Gentle breadcrumb trail.

Approaching
- More confident trail.

Located
- Orbiting particles around the player, indicating they are standing above the ore.

The goal is to make the player feel like they are following an instinct, not a waypoint.

## Design Rule

If the player has to stop and think about what the particles mean,
the guidance has failed.

The player should instinctively understand:

• Trail = Keep walking.

• Orbit = Dig here.
